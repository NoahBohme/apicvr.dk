Important: Advertising Protection Notice

All users of CVR-API must comply with the advertising protection rules under Danish CVR law.

Companies may opt out of advertising contact. As a user you commit to:

  • Respecting companies' wishes regarding advertising protection
  • Not using information about advertising-protected companies or production units for direct marketing, including phone calls, targeted mailings and similar activities
  • Clearly marking when passing data to third parties that a company or production unit is protected

These obligations follow from CVR Act § 19 and § 22. Violation of these rules may result in legal consequences.
